## Additional Notes for AI Generation:

- **Style Guidelines:** Photo-realistic 3D render, professional product photography aesthetic
- **Lighting:** Studio lighting with soft shadows, highlight the inflatable's form and texture
- **Background:** Pure transparent (alpha channel), no ground shadow
- **Quality:** High resolution, 2048x2048 or higher recommended
- **Perspective:** Consistent viewing angle across similar product types
- **Material:** Show inflatable texture and glossy PVC material appearance
- **Safety Features:** Visible safety netting, entrance/exit areas, proper proportions

## Recommended AI Tools:
- **Midjourney:** Add `--v 6 --style raw` for photorealistic results
- **DALL-E 3:** Emphasize "transparent background PNG" in prompt
- **Stable Diffusion:** Use ControlNet for consistent angles, mention "alpha channel"
